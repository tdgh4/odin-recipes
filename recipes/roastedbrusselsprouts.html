Roasted Brussel Sprouts

Description:

A soft and melt in your mouth delicious roasted brussel sprouts recipe that's so good, you wouldn't believe that it's healthy for you! This is so amazing that even I, a self-porclaimed vegetable hater, love eating! It's super easy to make, and honestly the hardest part is waiting for it to finish cooking in the oven

If you're not a fan of brussel sprouts, I can guarantee it's because your mom (or dad) boiled it to obivion in a pot on the stove for hours on end until it was a bland, squishy mess. This easy roasted brussel sprout recipe just needs you to rinse, cut in half, season, and pop in the oven. Easy peasy!

Ingredients:

  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Pepper Flakes (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
    2. Take the brussel sprouts and rinse them in water.
    3. Cut the brussel sprouts into halves. This makes them smaller and quicker to bake.
    4. Coat the brussel sprouts in a light layer of olive oil.
    5. Sprinkle a pinch of salt, pepper, garlic powder, and if you like some spice, chili pepper flakes.
    6. Pop into the oven for 30-40 minutes. About halfway through, flip them over for maximum crisp!
