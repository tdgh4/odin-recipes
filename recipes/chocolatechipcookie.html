Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

Do you love chocolate chip cookies as much as I do? These ooey-gooey chocolate chip cookies are amazing, soft in the middle with crisp edges.

You have to test out this delicious chocolate chip recipe. Once you try this sweet, soft, and crispy cookie, you'll never go back to eating prepackaged cookies. For our family, these don't last long, the hardest part is waiting for them to cool down after taking them out of the oven! So good that you'll have to make two batches. Or three. We promise we won't tattle if you broke your New Years Resolution!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 cups dark-chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the shortening, butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into the sugar mixture.
  5. Mix in the chocolate chips.
  6. Roll tablespoonfuls of dough into balls, and place them 2 inches apart onto the prepared cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden.
  8. Cool on a cooling rack for 5-10 minutes before digging in!
  9. ENJOY the deliciousness!
